Great question. Here's how our process works:

1. Initial Contact — Reach out via our Manufacturers page or contact form. Our team will confirm category fit and current evaluation capacity.

2. Product Submission — You ship product samples to our Ann Arbor lab. Our team determines whether clinical evaluation, laboratory testing, or both are appropriate.

3. Independent Evaluation — Our clinicians and researchers evaluate independently. You have no influence over findings or ratings.

4. Publication — Results are published in DENTAL ADVISOR regardless of outcome. This is what makes our ratings credible to clinicians.
