Our Capabilities

What We Test

Our Ann Arbor biomaterials lab is equipped to assess a wide range of physical and mechanical material properties — the metrics that matter for clinical performance and longevity.

🔗
Bond Strength

Shear and tensile bond strength to dentin, enamel, and various substrates under standardized protocols.

📡
Radiopacity

Measurement against aluminum step wedge standards to assess radiographic visibility and detection.

💪
Flexural Strength

Three-point bend testing to determine material strength and elastic modulus under simulated clinical load.

✨
Gloss & Hardness

Surface gloss retention after simulated wear and Vickers hardness measurement for polishability assessment.

📏
Film Thickness

Measurement of adhesive and cement film thickness under standardized load and substrate conditions.

🧪
Acid Resistance

Assessment of surface degradation and remineralization potential after acid challenge protocols.

🦠
Disinfectant Efficacy

Testing of infection control product effectiveness against relevant dental pathogens.

🖨️
3D-Print Materials

Physical property testing of 3D-printed dental restorations and materials for digital workflow applications.
