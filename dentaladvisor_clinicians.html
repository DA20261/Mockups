Getting the Most Out of DA

How Clinicians Use DENTAL ADVISOR

Whether you're evaluating a product before purchasing, earning CE credits, or staying current on clinical research — here's how to navigate.

Researching a Product

1
Go to Clinical Evaluations and search by product name, company, or category.
2
Filter by rating to narrow results to 4.5 or 4.0-rated products in your category.
3
Check award badges — Top Award and Long-Term Award winners have demonstrated sustained excellence.
4
Read the full evaluation (subscriber) for methodology, evaluator count, usage count, and detailed findings.
5
Compare alternatives in the same category to make a side-by-side decision.

Earning CE Credits

1
Go to Education and select Webinars to browse available CE courses.
2
Register for a live webinar or access on-demand recordings at your own pace.
3
Complete the post-course assessment to earn your CE certificate (1 credit per webinar).
4
Attend a Lab to Lecture event in your region for in-person CE and live product demonstrations.
5
Download CE certificates directly from your account dashboard.
