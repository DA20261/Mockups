The Process

From Submission to Publication

Our evaluation process is straightforward, transparent, and consistent. Here's exactly what to expect after you submit a product.

1
Submit

Contact our team with product details, samples, and supporting documentation.

2
Review

We confirm eligibility and assign your product to our evaluation queue.

3
Evaluate

Independent clinicians use your product under real-world conditions over an extended period.

4
Rate & Report

Evaluators submit standardized scores. Our team compiles findings into a formal evaluation report.

5
Publish

Results are published in DENTAL ADVISOR — in print and online — regardless of outcome.

Timeline: Most evaluations are completed within 3–6 months of product receipt, depending on the evaluation type and queue position. Lab-only evaluations typically complete faster than clinical evaluations.

How Scores Are Calculated
→Each evaluator submits a numerical score (0–100) at the end of the evaluation period
→Scores are aggregated across all evaluators for that product
→The cumulative percentage determines the rating designation (+++ to ++++½)
→Ratings are calculated automatically — not assigned by editorial judgment
→A minimum evaluator count is required for a rating to be published
What the Rating Covers
✓Handling and ease of use under clinical conditions
✓Clinical performance and predictability of results
✓Patient response and comfort
✓Overall recommendation — would the clinician use this product again?
✓Open-ended evaluator commentary (included in published report)

Common Questions

Manufacturer FAQs

Can we influence the evaluation outcome?

No. Once a product is submitted, our clinicians evaluate independently. We do not share preliminary results with manufacturers during the evaluation, and final ratings are not negotiable.

What happens if our product scores poorly?

We publish the evaluation regardless of outcome. This is non-negotiable — and it's the same policy that makes a high rating credible. If you're confident in your product, this shouldn't be a concern.

Do we have to pay for the evaluation?

Contact us directly for current fee information. Some evaluation types involve product submission costs; others do not. We can discuss the specifics based on your product type.

How many product samples do you need?

This varies by evaluation type. Clinical evaluations require enough samples for 32+ evaluators over an extended period. Laboratory evaluations require significantly fewer samples. We'll confirm quantities during the intake process.

Can we use the evaluation in our marketing?

Yes — once published, manufacturers may reference their DENTAL ADVISOR rating and link to the published evaluation. Award designations come with co-branded artwork and specific usage guidelines.

How long does the process take?

Clinical evaluations typically take 3–6 months from product receipt to publication. Laboratory-only evaluations are usually faster. We'll provide a timeline estimate during the intake process.

Can we submit a product that has already been reviewed?

If a product has been significantly reformulated, updated, or relaunched, it may be eligible for a new evaluation. Contact us to discuss.

Do you evaluate products outside North America?

Yes. We work with manufacturers globally. Products must meet applicable US regulatory requirements for clinical use in our evaluator network.
