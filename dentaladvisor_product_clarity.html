Clarity™ Aligners with Precision Grip Attachments received exceptional marks from our 32-member evaluator panel. Clinicians praised the material clarity and patient acceptance, with particular emphasis on the secure attachment retention the Precision Grip system provides over traditional button attachments.

"The Precision Grip attachment system genuinely changed how predictable my aligner cases feel. I've been able to tackle more complex cases with confidence." — Clinical Evaluator, General Dentist

The full evaluation report includes complete methodology, per-evaluator scoring distributions, clinical commentary across all 32 evaluators, statistical analysis with standard deviation data, and direct comparison to prior year results. Evaluators also provided detailed notes on patient compliance rates, attachment failure incidence, and overall treatment predictability across case complexity levels...
