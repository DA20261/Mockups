Award Categories

What Each Award Means

Six distinct award types — each representing a different level or type of evaluated excellence.

🏆
Top Product Award

Highest-rated products in their category, evaluated both clinically and in our biomaterials lab.

⭐
Preferred Product Award

Consistently strong performers recommended by our clinical evaluators across multiple uses.

🔬
Research Award

Products demonstrating superior laboratory performance metrics in our biomaterials testing.

✦
Editor's Choice

Standout products selected by our editorial team for exceptional overall value and innovation.

📅
Long-Term Award

Products evaluated over multiple years that continue to demonstrate sustained clinical excellence.

💡
Clinical Problem Solvers

Products that address a specific clinical challenge in a uniquely effective or innovative way.
