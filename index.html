Design Notes for Review
🎨Brand palette: Navy #203552 · Orange #E4762E · Gold #F8C569 · Charcoal #55565A · Fonts: Playfair Display + DM Sans
📱All pages are mobile responsive — resize your browser or view on a phone to see the mobile layout
🔍Search bar becomes sticky as you scroll down each page — it locks below the nav
🔒Subscriber gate is demonstrated on the Product Page — full evaluation text is blurred with a subscribe prompt
📅Award history accordion on the Product Page demonstrates Option C — badge + expandable year-by-year history
💰Sponsored ads on Homepage and Clinical Evaluations are clearly flagged as "Sponsored" to maintain editorial independence
🖼️Product images are placeholders — real product photography would be supplied by manufacturers
⚡These are static HTML mockups — interactive elements (search, filters) would be powered by Webflow CMS in the live build
