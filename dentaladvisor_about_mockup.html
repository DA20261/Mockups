Our Story

40+ Years of Independent Research

DENTAL ADVISOR was founded in 1983 on a single belief: that dental professionals deserve access to honest, unbiased product research — not manufacturer-funded claims dressed up as science.

Over four decades, we have published hundreds of clinical evaluations, laboratory research reports, and publications — all conducted independently, all published regardless of outcome.

No sponsor has ever influenced our findings. No advertiser has ever shaped our ratings. That independence is the foundation of everything we do.

"The moment you let advertisers influence your findings, you stop being research and start being marketing."
— Dr. Sabiha Bunek, DDS, Founder

Meet Our Founders

Dr. John Powers, PhD
Biomaterials Research Director & Co-Founder

Dr. John Powers is one of the most respected figures in dental biomaterials research in the United States. His career spans over four decades of rigorous, independent laboratory investigation into the physical and mechanical properties of dental materials — the kind of foundational science that separates evidence-based clinical decisions from marketing claims.

As co-founder and Biomaterials Research Director at DENTAL ADVISOR, Dr. Powers has overseen every laboratory evaluation the organization has ever published. His protocols — developed and refined over decades — form the scientific backbone of DENTAL ADVISOR's evaluation methodology, and his commitment to reproducibility and transparency has set the standard for independent dental product research in the industry.

Dr. Powers earned his PhD from the University of Michigan and spent a distinguished academic career there before channeling his expertise into building a research organization that could serve practicing clinicians directly — not just the academic community. That mission, begun in 1983, continues today in every evaluation DENTAL ADVISOR publishes.

"Good materials science isn't about finding the best product — it's about finding the truth about every product. Clinicians deserve nothing less."

— Dr. John Powers, PhD
